About Me

I am a design-minded, detail oriented developer with a deep love for inquiry and creating data-driven, connected products. I have a background in scientific research, in both biochemistry and social psychology where, I developed a deep love for designing experiments to address important questions, and working on cross-disciplinary teams to deliver results. Since transitioning to web development, I have enjoyed bridging the gap between engineering and design--building systems to analyze, store, interact, and display data--and feel equally at home on the frontend as on the backend. I am seeking opportunities where I can leverage the expertise I have gained in technologies such as Node, Express, React, and AWS to create fullstack, cloud-supported applications with elegant and seamless UIs that connect, empower, and inspire.
